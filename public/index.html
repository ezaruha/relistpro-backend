Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RelistPro.

What is RelistPro?
RelistPro is a Chrome extension and Telegram bot that helps Vinted sellers repost their listings faster. It automatically creates fresh listings with edited photos, deletes old ones, and tracks your wardrobe analytics.
Is it safe to use?
Yes. RelistPro uses your existing Vinted session cookies — it never asks for or stores your Vinted password. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We are fully GDPR compliant.
Does Vinted allow reposting?
Reposting is a common practice among Vinted sellers. RelistPro applies subtle photo edits so each repost appears as a unique listing. Many successful sellers use reposting as a core sales strategy.
How does photo editing work?
RelistPro applies invisible edits — subtle rotation, colour shifts, pixel noise, and metadata changes — so Vinted's duplicate detection doesn't flag your reposted listing. The photos look identical to the human eye.
What happens to my old listing?
When you repost, RelistPro creates the new listing first, then deletes the old one automatically. A backup of the original is always saved so you can restore it if needed.
Can I use it on my phone?
Yes! The RelistPro Telegram Bot lets you list and repost items by sending photos from your phone. The bot uses AI to analyse your photos and create listings automatically.
What Vinted countries are supported?
RelistPro works on all 25+ Vinted domains including UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Poland, Czech Republic, Belgium, and more.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from the Account tab in the extension dashboard, or via the Stripe billing portal. Your paid features remain active until the end of your billing period.
Is my data safe?
Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database with encrypted connections. We never share your data with third parties. You can export all your data as CSV or delete your account at any time.
How do I get help?
Reply to any email from us, use the Telegram bot's /help command, or contact us at support@relistpro.com. Pro plan users get priority support.
